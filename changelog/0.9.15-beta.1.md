## Videorc lives at videorc.com

Every link the app opens — your account, sign-in, billing, the changelog —
and the update feed itself now point at videorc.com. If you bookmarked the
old videorc-web.vercel.app address, it keeps working, but videorc.com is
home now.

## Notes stops hiding your app

Opening the Notes window with "keep on top" enabled sent the entire app
behind your other windows until Notes closed — a macOS window-layering
quirk. Notes (and Comments, and Captions) now float without hiding
anything.

## Fixes

- The Library "…" actions menu sizes to its labels — no more two-line
  "Open in Publish" fragments.
- The Comments window title cleared out from under the macOS traffic
  lights.
- Removed the red "Production preview requires native CAMetalLayer" badge:
  it fired for harmless in-between states and explained nothing. The
  preview window itself now reports and heals its own issues, and the
  Studio badge only shows states you can act on.
