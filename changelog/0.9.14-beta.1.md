## Comments window works again

The detached Comments window — your second-monitor live-chat reader — broke
when it received a saved session's transcript. It renders everything again,
live or from the Library.

## Controls that say what they mean

- Source pickers never sit empty anymore: they say "Finding devices…", "No
  devices found — check System Access in Settings", or "Saved device
  unavailable — pick another" — whatever is actually going on.
- Compact controls in the Studio show short, readable values like
  "2K · 1440p30" instead of truncated dimensions.
- Settings shows the full name of each macOS permission target, so you know
  exactly what to grant.

## Quieter, better-placed notifications

Notifications sit above the bottom command bar instead of on top of it, and
repeated "source unavailable" warnings collapse into a single message. When
Videorc swaps a capture source automatically, the details stay visible in
Sources until you've seen them.

## Light theme contrast pass

Darker secondary text, clearer borders, and more visible controls — the
light theme now reads at a glance.

## Fixes

- A rare crash dialog ("write EPIPE") when the app's logging pipe went away
  can no longer take Videorc down.
