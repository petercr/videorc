## Shape your camera

The screen + camera scene's bubble now comes in three shapes: **Rectangle**,
**Rounded** — a rounded square by default, with a corner-radius slider from
sharp to pill — and the existing **Circle**.

And the bubble's framing is yours too: **Wide**, **Square**, or **Portrait**.
Portrait center-crops your camera into a tall frame — the vertical-camera look —
so position yourself centered. Pick all of it in the Scene tab's camera
inspector; changes apply live, and the scene stage diagram shows the true
shape.

Every shape and framing renders identically in the preview window and the
finished recording.

## Fixes

- Recording with the preview window closed no longer shows a misleading
  "Blocked" health warning in the Studio.
